Our Favorites

Our six most-ordered dishes, straight from the smokestack.

  • 12-Hour Brisket

    Oak-smoked overnight, hand-sliced to order, and finished with a rub we've been refining for a decade. Served with pickles, slaw, and a slice of cornbread.

  • St. Louis Ribs

    Dry-rubbed with our signature spice blend and slow-smoked low over hickory until the meat pulls clean off the bone. No sauce needed — but we won't stop you.

  • The Iron Burger

    Two hand-packed patties seared hard over open flame, stacked with smoked bacon, aged cheddar, whiskey-caramelized onions, and house sauce on a toasted brioche bun.

  • Smoked Wings

    Dry-rubbed, smoked for two hours, then flash-fried for that perfect crispy bite. Choose your heat: hot honey, bourbon BBQ, or Nashville-style dry rub.

  • Smoked Mac

    Three-cheese mac — sharp cheddar, smoked gouda, and parmesan — finished in the smoker with a crunchy buttered breadcrumb top. Comfort food with a backbone.

  • Bourbon Chocolate

    Dense flourless chocolate cake drizzled with bourbon caramel and finished with flaky sea salt. Rich, boozy, and just the right amount of sweet to close out the meal.
